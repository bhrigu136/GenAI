# **Tokens & Tokenization**
LLMs ya computers kabhi bhi text ko nahi smajh sakte hai becuse Our computers works on the binary Systems

Who is PM of India? --->breaks into multiple chunks-->["Who", "is", "PM", "of", "India"] ---this list of strings called 
as Tokens --- and each tokens are assign some number called as token ids-- and LLMs have dictionaries which have some 
values of token ids are already pre-assign ----and LLMs understand it and again it assign some set of numbers and 
again these set of numbers are are mapped in dictionaries and by combining this words it give output to the users > 
this process is called as Tokenization and the process of convertion tokens into strings is called as Tokeniser.
